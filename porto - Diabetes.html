Portfolio Detail

The Diabetes Predictor Project is an innovative and user-friendly tool designed to empower individuals with a proactive approach towards their health. This web-based application provides early predictions for the risk of developing diabetes by analyzing various health parameters, including the number of pregnancies, blood glucose levels, diastolic blood pressure, and more. The goal of this project is to enable users to make informed decisions about their health and lifestyle choices, ultimately reducing the incidence of diabetes through early intervention.
