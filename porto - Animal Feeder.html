Portfolio Detail

Smart Feeder is a groundbreaking IoT-based application designed to simplify the task of feeding your pets, such as dogs and cats, even when you are swamped with a busy schedule. Recognizing the importance of regularly feeding our beloved pets, we have developed a web-based platform that serves several key functions: scheduling pet feedings and monitoring the remaining pet food stock. With Smart Feeder, you can ensure your pets are well-fed and happy, regardless of your daily routine.
